# Agent Incident Log

> Structured records of errors, ambiguity, and blockers encountered during pipeline execution.
> Agents append entries when they cannot proceed or spend excessive time on a task.
> This log drives documentation self-healing — review periodically to fix root causes.

## Entry Format

```
---
timestamp: YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM
agent: @fabric-advisor | @fabric-architect | @fabric-reviewer | @fabric-tester | @fabric-engineer | @fabric-documenter
project: <workspace-name>
phase: Discovery | Design | Review | Plan | Deploy | Validate | Document
step: <specific task or action being attempted>
duration_minutes: <time spent before recording this entry>
severity: blocker | ambiguity | delay
problem: |
  <description of what went wrong or was unclear>
resolution: |
  <how it was resolved, or "unresolved" if escalated>
affected_docs: |
  <list of files that should be updated to prevent recurrence>
---
```
